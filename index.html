About Me

Welcome to my official website! I am Imran H, an aspiring writer with a passion for storytelling. "Devil's Curse" is my first web novel, set in the Great Sengoku Period, exploring themes of honor, vengeance, and supernatural battles.

My Novel

Synopsis: Set during the tumultuous Great Sengoku Period (1460s-1560s CE), a desperate clan member sought to gain supernatural powers by negotiating with demons sealed away by the legendary samurai Raiden Takeshi. His actions shattered the seal, unleashing demons that dominated and enslaved humanity, forcing teenagers to become curse samurai who gain demonic powers only to serve their leaders.
