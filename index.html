About Me

Welcome! I am Imran H, an aspiring author with a passion for storytelling. *Devil's Curse* is my debut novel, a thrilling tale set in the Great Sengoku Period. This story explores themes of honor, vengeance, and supernatural battles.

About My Novel

Title: Devil's Curse

Synopsis: During the Great Sengoku Period, a desperate act releases ancient demons, enslaving humanity and forcing teenagers to become cursed samurai. Amidst this chaos, 17-year-old Ryuji embarks on a perilous journey for revenge and redemption.
